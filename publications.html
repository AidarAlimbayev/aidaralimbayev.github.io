Test stand for prospective mobile power-supply sources

A. S. Alimbaev, V. S. Uss, A. B. Mirmanov, P. P. Shumakov

This article describes a method for long-term testing of storage batteries and high-capacity ion sources using National Instruments technologies. We developed a test stand with a DAQ NI USB-6001 and provide a brief description of the LabVIEW program, including the block diagram for correcting temperature coefficients. The virtual interface for monitoring charge and discharge is shown. During bench tests, capacity degradation was confirmed: the ionistor capacitance fell from 50 kF to 3.3 kF over two weeks of testing and continued to decrease with each charge–discharge cycle.
